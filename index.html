One human, one health record.

Imagine if every American had their health record available in the palm of their hand.

The Standard Health Record Initiative is working to create a single, high-quality health record for every individual in the United States.

Our vision is to fundamentally shift how health data is digitally recorded and shared to improve patient outcomes. When adopted, the SHR data model solves semantic interoperability for healthcare. As an open source project, the SHR engages a broad community and can provide a powerful model for global health.

Key Benefits of the SHR

  • Centralized health dataAn aggregated, secure source of high quality health data.
  • Patient-focusedA simple, portable way for individuals to share their health data.
  • 24/7 AccessibilityIndividuals, caregivers, and clinicians have the right data at the right time, anywhere.
  • Added SecurityBiometric identification (e.g., a fingerprint) in case of an emergency.

The Standard Health Record Will:

Protect the security and privacy of patient health information.

Reduce waste, fraud, and abuse in healthcare delivery systems.

Provide secure, informed-consent access to patient health data across multiple platforms.

Allow modern healthcare systems and processes to improve the patient’s healthcare experience.

Empower the patient to add their own data, verify existing data, and share their data with trusted third parties.

Provide accessiblity by patients and authorized healthcare providers to the patient’s health-record world-wide.
